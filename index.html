04 — Questions

Things people actually ask.

How long does a typical build take? +
Most projects launch within 2–4 weeks of kickoff. Simpler chatbots can go live in a week. Larger custom systems with multiple integrations take 4–6. We'll give you a firm timeline after the discovery call.
What does it cost? +
Builds typically range from a few hundred to several thousand dollars depending on scope. We price every project up-front so you know exactly what you're paying — no surprises, no monthly creep. Some clients prefer a small monthly retainer for ongoing tweaks; others just pay once and own it.
Do I need to switch tools? +
Almost never. We build around the tools you already use — your CRM, calendar, phone system, email, ads. The whole point is to make your existing stack quieter and faster, not replace it.
What happens if the AI gets something wrong? +
Every system has guardrails and human-in-the-loop hand-offs for edge cases. The chatbot doesn't pretend to know things it doesn't — it loops you in. We also monitor and refine for the first 30 days post-launch to catch anything we missed.
Will my customers know it's AI? +
We're transparent by default — disclosure usually builds trust. But the bigger story is that customers don't really care, as long as they got the answer fast and got booked in. The complaints are about slow responses, not AI ones.
Do you work with small businesses? +
Yes — that's most of our work. Solo operators, family businesses, small teams of 2–20. The smaller the team, the more dramatic the time savings tend to be.
